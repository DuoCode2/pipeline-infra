# Malaysia Market Rules

## Price & Currency
- Format: `RM12.90` (no space between RM and amount)
- Never use MYR, $, or other symbols

## Phone
- International: `+60 12-345 6789`
- Local: `012-345 6789`
- WhatsApp is the PRIMARY contact method (use `https://wa.me/60XXXXXXXXX`)

## Address
- Format: Street, City, Postcode State
- Example: 12 Jalan Bukit Bintang, Kuala Lumpur, 55100 Wilayah Persekutuan

## Halal
- If JAKIM-certified: show Halal badge prominently (hero + trust bar)
- If unclear: do NOT claim Halal

## Languages (ALL 4 required)
| Locale | Tone | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| `en` | Warm, confident, use contractions | Show, don't tell |
| `ms` | Semi-formal BM, natural rhythm | Keep original names (Nasi Lemak stays BM) |
| `zh-CN` | Concise, bilingual names | Nasi Lemak 讀ｰ豬㋠･ｭ, fullwidth punctuation |
| `zh-TW` | Same clarity, traditional chars | Hong Kong/Taiwan nuances |

## Bilingual Content
- Menu/service items: keep original name + translation in Chinese
- Example: `Nasi Lemak Ayam Goreng 讀ｰ豬㋠･ｭ轤ｸ鮑｡`
