# Service Industry Reference

Design guidelines for general service businesses: plumbing, electrical, cleaning, accounting, legal, consulting, tuition, repair, and other professional service providers.

---

## Color Strategy

- **Palette mood:** Professional, trustworthy, competent. Neutral base with purposeful accent.
- **Primary approach:** Neutral professional tones — navy, charcoal, deep grey as base. One brand accent for CTAs and highlights.
- **Backgrounds:** White or light grey (`#F8F9FA`). Clean and uncluttered — services sell on trust, not visual excitement.
- **Accent usage:** Single accent color from `brand-colors.json` for CTAs, checkmarks, and key highlights. Restraint is key.
- **Avoid:** Overly colorful or playful palettes (unless it's a children's service), dark/moody themes, neon accents.
- **Trust section:** Light blue or light green background for credentials and guarantees — psychologically associated with reliability.

## Typography

- **Display font:** Raleway, Source Sans Pro, or Libre Franklin — clean, professional, no-nonsense.
- **Body font:** Lato, Open Sans, or Nunito Sans — excellent readability, approachable but professional.
- **Service names:** H3 (24–28px), semibold. Price or "from RM" inline if applicable.
- **Process steps:** H3 for step titles, Body for descriptions. Number or icon prefix for each step.

## Component Emphasis (Ranked)

1. **Service List** — Most critical. Complete list of services offered, grouped by category. Each service: name, brief description (1–2 lines), price or price range in RM if applicable.
2. **Process / How It Works** — 3–5 step visual process. "1. Contact us → 2. Get a quote → 3. We do the work → 4. You approve". Reduces friction and uncertainty.
3. **Reviews / Testimonials** — 3–5 client quotes with specific service mentioned. Credibility is everything for service businesses.
4. **CTA / Get a Quote** — Clear call-to-action for inquiry. WhatsApp primary, phone secondary. "Get a Free Quote" framing.
5. **Credentials / Certifications** — Professional licenses, insurance, years of experience, number of clients served.
6. **Service Area** — Map or list of areas served. Critical for location-based services.

## Section Order Override

```
Hero (professional photo + value proposition + Get Quote CTA)
  → Service List (categorized with descriptions)
  → How It Works (step-by-step process)
  → Reviews (client testimonials)
  → Credentials + Trust Badges (licenses, experience stats)
  → Service Area (map or area list)
  → CTA Section (standalone, contrasting background)
  → Contact + Hours (WhatsApp, phone, operating hours)
  → Footer
```

## Copy Tone

- **Hero headline examples:**
  - EN: "Reliable Plumbing Services Across Klang Valley"
  - MS: "Perkhidmatan Paip Dipercayai di Seluruh Lembah Klang"
  - zh-CN: "巴生谷值得信赖的水管服务"
  - zh-TW: "巴生谷值得信賴的水管服務"
- **Value-first messaging:** Lead with what the customer gets, not what the business does. "A clean home without lifting a finger" not "We provide cleaning services".
- **Trust signals in copy:** Weave credentials naturally. "With over 10 years serving PJ and Subang, we've fixed 5,000+ air-cond units" — specific and verifiable.
- **Process copy:** Active, simple. "Tell us what you need. We'll quote you within an hour. Our team arrives on time, every time."
- **Avoid:** Vague claims ("quality service"), corporate jargon ("holistic solutions"), over-promising ("100% guaranteed satisfaction").

## Malaysia-Specific Notes

- **Response time:** Malaysian consumers value fast response. If the business offers same-day or emergency services, highlight "Respon dalam 1 jam" (Response within 1 hour) prominently.
- **WhatsApp-first:** Service inquiries in Malaysia happen overwhelmingly via WhatsApp. Make the WhatsApp CTA the primary action — above phone calls and definitely above email.
- **Service area clarity:** Malaysia's Klang Valley has many townships. Be specific: "Serving Petaling Jaya, Subang Jaya, Shah Alam, and Klang" rather than just "Klang Valley".
- **Pricing transparency:** Show price ranges where possible. "Aircond servicing from RM80" is better than "Contact for pricing". Malaysian consumers will WhatsApp competitors who show prices.
- **Languages spoken:** For service businesses, mention if the team speaks multiple languages. "Our technicians speak BM, English, and Mandarin."
- **Licensed and insured:** If applicable, mention "Berlesen & Berinsurans" (Licensed & Insured). This is a significant trust factor for home services.
- **No-obligation quote:** Frame the first contact as free and no-obligation. "WhatsApp for a free quote — no commitment needed."
- **Payment flexibility:** "Cash, online transfer, and DuitNow accepted" — mention available payment methods as Malaysian consumers increasingly prefer cashless options.
