## Usage Notes

- **Delivery links**: For `menu-order` archetype, link to the platforms relevant to the business's region. In Malaysia, default to GrabFood + FoodPanda.
- **E-commerce links**: For `ecommerce-catalog` archetype, link to the business's Shopee/Lazada store if available.
- **Contact method**: Use the region's dominant messaging platform as the primary contact CTA. WhatsApp is the default for most of Southeast Asia, Middle East, and Latin America.
- **Maps**: Google Maps is the universal default. Use Waze for driving directions in Malaysia. In regions where Google Maps is blocked or uncommon (CN, KR), use the local alternative.
- **Reviews**: Always show Google rating if available. Supplement with regional review platforms where relevant.
